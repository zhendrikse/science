## Electric dipole field
<div class="header_line"><br/></div>

Electric field
$$\vec{E} ( \vec{r} ) = -\dfrac {1} {4\pi\epsilon_0} \nabla \bigg( \dfrac{\vec{r} \cdot \vec{p}} {r^3} \bigg)\text{, with }\vec{p}=+q(\vec{r}_+) + -q(\vec{r}_-)$$
