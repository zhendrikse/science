## Polar coordinates
<div class="header_line"><br/></div>

| Parameter | Symbol | Meaning                       | Range   |
|-----------| ------ |-------------------------------|---------|
| `r`       | r      | radius                        | ≥ 0     |
| `polar`   | θ      | angle with respect to +z-axis | [0, π]  |
| `azimuth` | φ      | angle in x-y plane            | [0, 2π) |
